About

333 SENSATION is a Dutch industrial techno project driven by raw, low-BPM soundscapes. Inspired by the grit of underground circuits — pounding kicks, corroded textures, and walls of distorted noise.

Besides producing, I work as a visual artist (media & design), where sound and image converge in a dark, industrial aesthetic.
